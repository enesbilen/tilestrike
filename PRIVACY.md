# TileStrike Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

TileStrike does not collect personal data.

## Accounts

TileStrike does not require user accounts, sign-in, or registration.

## Analytics and Tracking

TileStrike does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

## Local Game Data

Game scores and progress may be stored locally on your device. This information is not sent to the developer or to any external server.
