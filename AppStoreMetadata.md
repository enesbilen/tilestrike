## Description

TileStrike, satir ve sutun temizleme uzerine kurulu hizli, akici ve stratejik bir blok bulmaca oyunudur. Alttaki parcalari panoya yerlestir, dolan satir veya sutunlari patlat, kombo zincirleri kur ve rekorunu gelistir.

TileStrike'i farkli kilan sey sadece blok yerlestirmek degil, oynayis tarzini degistiren modlardir:

- Klasik: Dengeli ve sade blok bulmaca deneyimi.
- Bomb Rush: Daha sik gelen bomba parcalariyla panoyu agresif sekilde ac.
- Zen: Sikistiginda kurtarici tekli parca ile daha rahat oyna.
- Magnet: Cizgi patladiktan sonra kalan bloklar merkeze dogru toplanir; her hamle sonrasini dusunmen gerekir.

Oyunda ekranina uyum saglayan dinamik pano, bomba parcalari, kombo puanlari, skor gecmisi, tema secenekleri, ses ve titresim ayarlari bulunur.

Ozellikler:

- 4 farkli oyun modu
- Dinamik pano boyutu
- Bomba parcalari
- Kombo puan sistemi
- Magnet modunda merkez cekim mekanigi
- Klasik, Neon ve Ocean tema secenekleri
- Klasik, Wood ve Metallic blok stili secenekleri
- Ses ve titresim kontrolu
- Yerel skor gecmisi
- Hesap veya giris gerektirmez

TileStrike, kisa molalarda hizli oynamak isteyenler icin kolay baslar; fakat modlar ve kombo stratejileriyle ustalasmayi seven oyunculara da alan acar.

## App Review Response Draft

Hello,

Thank you for the review. We updated TileStrike to further differentiate the app with distinct gameplay systems, settings, and presentation changes.

In this build, we added:

- A dedicated Settings screen with sound and haptic controls.
- Three visual themes: Classic, Neon, and Ocean.
- Three block styles: Classic, Wood, and Metallic.
- Four gameplay modes: Classic, Bomb Rush, Zen, and Magnet.
- Bomb Rush mode, where bomb pieces appear more often and line clears receive a scoring bonus.
- Zen mode, where the game can provide a rescue single tile when the player has no available move.
- Magnet mode, a distinct post-clear board mechanic where remaining blocks are pulled toward the center after a line clear.
- A visible selected-mode badge on the main menu.
- Interstitial ads after game over with frequency limits to avoid interrupting normal gameplay.

These changes alter both the app presentation and the gameplay rules, especially with Magnet mode and the new mode system. We believe this version provides a more distinct TileStrike experience and is not a simple repackaged block puzzle template.

Thank you for your time and consideration.
